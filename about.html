About me

My name is Narciso Jaramillo, but everyone calls me "nj" because it's easier.

I work at Adobe doing a mix of development, design, and product management. (Of course, opinions on this site are all my own.)

Most recently, I started and help lead the Brackets project, a free, open-source code editor for front-end web development. I also help guide other projects in the Web Platform and Authoring group at Adobe.

In earlier roles, I worked on Dreamweaver (v1.0 through MX), Contribute (v1 through v2), Flash Builder (v2 through v4), Flash Catalyst (R.I.P.), and mobile features for Flex. For fun, I also built an RSS ticker called Snackr.

In my spare time, I play jazz.

I'm @notwebsafe on Twitter and njx on GitHub.
